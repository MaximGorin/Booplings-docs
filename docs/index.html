Booplings

A playful macOS companion that reacts to your clicks and keystrokes

What is Booplings?

Booplings is a lightweight desktop buddy designed to bring a bit of joy and presence to your everyday Mac experience. It’s not a productivity tool or a distraction — it’s a quiet, animated companion that lives on your screen and responds to your interactions.

How it Works

Booplings reacts to your mouse clicks and keyboard presses with cute animations, small movements, or subtle reactions. Everything happens locally. There’s no sign-in, no account, no tracking. You open your Mac, and Booplings is just... there — ready to react, observe, and boop.

Why It Exists

Most apps want your attention. Booplings doesn’t. It just wants to exist alongside you — to be present, ambient, alive. It’s an experiment in warmth and minimalism: what if software didn’t demand anything, but simply responded to you?

Your Privacy

Booplings doesn’t collect or transmit any data. Preferences (like character, position, and size) are stored only on your device. Click and keystroke counts are tracked locally, and never leave your Mac. You can read more here: Privacy Policy.

Want to Get in Touch?

We’re not a company. There’s no support team or ticket system. But if you want to share feedback, ideas, or just say hi — we’d love to hear from you: Contact & Support
